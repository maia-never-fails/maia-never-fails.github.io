Maia's Malevolent
Human Repellant

Required Ingredients

  • 6th Grade humor
  • your brother's jersey after a game (if necessary can be substituted with sock after a hike)
  • mug of half-drunk tea that's been sitting in your room since you were sick 3 weeks ago
  • a charger cable that only works at a 47° angle and emotional distress
  • a pen that works perfectly except when you actually need it to

Step #1

  1. First place the mug in the center of your set up, drip 8 drops of sweat from the jersey into it
  2. Spend 6 or 7 minutes using 6th grade humor to try to cheer up the mug
  3. Once you meet inevitable failure, stir the contents of the cup with the pen
  4. Aggressively take apart the pen and add its contents of a nearly full ink cartridge
  5. Rip a part of the jersey, try to set it on fire using the cable
  6. Once you get to a climax of frustration, spit into the mug

Optional Ingredients

  • an unread notification that you are actively avoiding like if you read it your life will end
  • a notebook where you've written exactly one motivational sentence and then gave up on yourself
  • a chair that squeaks like a fart every time you shift
  • vegemite (no explanation necessary)

Step #2 (Optional)

  1. If you'd like a particularly potent solution follow these next instructions
  2. Rip out that page of nonsense in that notebook and grind it into a fine powder then add it to the mug
  3. Whisper at least 12 cheesy inspirational quotes directly into the brim of the mug
  4. Burn the chair into ashes and add 1827 grains of ash to the mixture (the potion will explode if this number is not accurate)
  5. Add a spoonful of vegemite
  6. Let the mug sit watching the news for 7 years straight

Excellent Situations For Use

  1. When you're overwhelmed
  2. When your creepy uncle wants to give you a hug
  3. When someone you don't like tries to approach you
  4. When you want to perform an Irish goodbye
  5. When you're mid excellent movie and someone wants anything from you
  6. When you want to cry for 3 hours straight
